Common Miele Vacuum Repairs

Loss of Suction

Clogged filters, blocked hoses, worn seals, or motor issues. We diagnose and fix all suction problems.

Motor Repair

Strange noises, burning smell, or complete motor failure. Expert motor diagnosis and replacement.

Powerhead Issues

Brushroll not spinning, belt replacement, electrical connections. Complete powerhead service.

Hose & Wand Repair

Cracked hoses, broken wands, electrical cord damage. Replacement with genuine Miele parts.

Battery Service

Cordless vacuum battery replacement for Triflex and other battery-powered models.

Filter Replacement

HEPA filter service, exhaust filters, and motor protection filters for all models.
