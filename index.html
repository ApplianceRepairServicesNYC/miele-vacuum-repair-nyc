Miele Vacuum Models We Service

Complete C3 Series

Full-size canister vacuums with powerful suction. We repair motors, hoses, powerheads, and all electronic components.

Loss of Suction: Clogged HEPA filters, blocked hoses, worn seals, or motor issues. We diagnose and restore full suction power to your Complete C3.

Motor Repair: Strange noises, burning smell, or complete motor failure. Expert motor diagnosis and replacement with genuine Miele motors.

Powerhead Service: Brushroll not spinning, belt replacement, electrical connections. Complete powerhead and electrobrush repair.

Hose & Wand Repair: Cracked hoses, broken wands, cord retractor issues. All replacements use genuine Miele parts.

Filter Replacement: HEPA filter service, exhaust filters, and motor protection filters. We service Marin, Kona, Calima, Brilliant, and all C3 variants.

Classic C1 Series

Compact canister vacuums perfect for smaller spaces. Expert repair for all C1 models and accessories.

Loss of Suction: Clogged filters, blocked hoses, worn seals, or motor degradation. We restore full suction power to your Classic C1.

Motor Repair: Motor diagnostics for strange noises, overheating, or failure. Expert motor replacement service.

Cable Rewind: Cord retractor repair and replacement. Fix stuck or non-retracting power cords.

Filter Maintenance: AirClean filter service, dustbag holder repair, and exhaust filter replacement.

We service Pure Suction, Turbo Team, and Cat & Dog C1 variants throughout NYC with same-day appointments available.

Compact C2 Series

Mid-size canister vacuums with excellent filtration. We service all Compact C2 variants including Electro+ and Cat & Dog.

Loss of Suction: Suction control issues, clogged filters, or airflow blockages. Full diagnostic and repair service.

Motor Repair: Motor diagnostics, overheating issues, and complete motor replacement when needed.

Powerhead Issues: Electrobrush motor repair, belt replacement, and electrical connection service.

Hose & Wand: Telescopic wand problems, hose cracks, and wand connection repairs.

Filter Service: AirClean filter system, exhaust filter maintenance, and HEPA upgrades. We service C2 Electro+, Cat & Dog, and Hard Floor models.

Triflex HX1 Series

Cordless stick vacuums with 3-in-1 design. Battery replacement, motor repair, and full diagnostic service.

Battery Service: Battery replacement and reconditioning for cordless operation. Restore full runtime to your Triflex.

Loss of Suction: Suction power loss diagnosis, airflow optimization, and dust container seal repair.

Motor Repair: Brushroll motor failure, main motor issues, and complete motor replacement.

Powerhead Issues: Brushroll replacement, belt service, and electrobrush motor repair.

Filter Replacement: HEPA filter service and dust container maintenance. We service all Triflex Pro, Cat & Dog, and Runner models.

Blizzard CX1 Series

Bagless canister vacuums with Vortex Technology. We repair cyclone systems, filters, and all components.

Loss of Suction: Vortex cyclone system cleaning, suction power optimization, and airflow restoration.

Motor Repair: Motor protection issues, overheating diagnosis, and motor replacement service.

Powerhead Issues: Powerhead connectivity, brushroll service, and electrical connection repair.

Filter Replacement: Gore CleanStream filter service, self-cleaning filter maintenance, and Hygiene Lifetime filter care.

Dust Container: Release mechanism repair, seal replacement, and emptying system service. Expert service for Excellence, Cat & Dog, and Turbo Team models.

Scout RX3 Robot

Intelligent robot vacuums with smart navigation. Sensor repair, battery service, and software diagnostics.

Battery Service: Battery replacement and charging contact cleaning. Restore full runtime to your Scout robot.

Loss of Suction: Suction motor service, dustbin seal repair, and airflow optimization.

Motor Repair: Main drive motor, suction motor, and brushroll motor diagnostics and replacement.

Sensor Service: Navigation sensor calibration, cliff sensor repair, and obstacle detection fixes.

Connectivity: WiFi issues, app pairing problems, and software diagnostics. We service Scout RX3 Home Vision and all Scout generations.

Dynamic U1 Uprights

Powerful upright vacuums with swivel neck. Complete service for brushrolls, belts, motors, and filtration systems.

Loss of Suction: Sealed system restoration, clogged filters, and airflow blockage diagnosis.

Motor Repair: Main motor and brushroll motor replacement. Strange noises and overheating fixes.

Powerhead Issues: Belt replacement, brushroll service, height adjustment repair, and headlight replacement.

Hose & Wand: Handle lock repair, swivel neck mechanism, and wand connection service.

Filter Replacement: HEPA filter service, bag change indicator repair, and exhaust filter maintenance. We service AutoEco, Cat & Dog, Jazz, Twist, and HomeCare U1 models.

Boost CX1 Series

High-performance bagless vacuums. Expert repair for all Boost models including motor and suction issues.

Loss of Suction: Mono-cyclone technology service, suction power optimization, and airflow restoration.

Motor Repair: Motor diagnostics, overheating issues, and complete motor replacement service.

Powerhead Issues: Powerhead connectivity, brushroll motor service, and belt replacement.

Filter Replacement: Fine dust filter replacement, self-cleaning filter mechanism repair, and Hygiene Lifetime filter service.

Dust Container: Emptying mechanism repair, seal replacement, and release button fixes. All repairs use genuine Miele parts.

Cat & Dog Series

Specialized pet hair vacuums with turbo brushes. Filter replacement, odor control service, and deep cleaning maintenance.

Loss of Suction: Pet hair clog removal, suction optimization for pet homes, and airflow restoration.

Motor Repair: Turbobrush motor service, main motor diagnostics, and motor replacement for heavy pet hair use.

Powerhead Issues: Electrobrush restoration, belt replacement, and brushroll service for pet hair pickup.

Filter Replacement: Active AirClean filter replacement, charcoal odor filter service, and HEPA filter maintenance.

Odor Control: Charcoal filter replacement and deep cleaning service. We service all Cat & Dog models across Complete, Compact, Classic, and Blizzard lines.
